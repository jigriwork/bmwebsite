Shipping & Returns

Last updated: March 2026

Shipping

Brand Mark primarily serves customers through our physical store in Berhampur. For delivery availability, timelines, and charges, please contact our team directly on WhatsApp.

  • Shipping availability may vary by location.
  • Estimated delivery times are shared at the time of order confirmation.
  • Delays due to courier/network disruptions may occasionally occur.

Returns & Exchanges

We support genuine exchanges/returns as per product condition and billing details.

  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and with original tags/invoice.
  • Requests should be initiated within the communicated return window.
  • Innerwear, perfumes, and select items may be non-returnable for hygiene/safety reasons.
  • Refund/exchange mode depends on product category and billing type.

Need Help?

For any shipping, exchange, or return support, please contact us via WhatsApp.
